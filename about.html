My Journey

My Home Country: South Africa

I was born and raised in South Africa. The past 19 years of my life in South Africa made me the person I am today. It will forever be my home. South Africa is known as a Rainbow Nation, which means that we are a nation filled with many people from different cultural backgrounds. We are also known for our beautiful nature, which attracts tourists from all over the world. Our diversity, unity and nature makes me proudly South African. Although South Africa will forever be in my heart, I decided to take a big step to change my life completely...I packed my bags and moved to Switzerland.

My Studies: Austria

Once again, I have decided to change my life and move to another country, leaving a wonderful family behind. I decided to study Informatics at the IMC Krems University of Applied Sciences. After months of looking at hundreds of universities, I came accross this university.
